Generative art


Quite recently, I began to appreciate a unique domain intersecting the worlds of coding and art - generative art. To simplify things a lot, generative art represents the involvement of a human-independent system (such as an algorithm) in a creative process. Such a system is a key element within the creative process, including its outputs. Be it paintings, songs, novels, or sculptures. If you would like to have a better overview, the following article on Wikipedia could be a place to start.

Now that you know what to expect, take a peek at “my” pieces if you like.
